FAQ

Why have a hot tub?

Just imagine it... relaxing in gloriously-warm bubbling water under the winter stars or getting into the summer party mood with friends or simply enjoying a great experience together with your family. Whatever party, celebration or get-together you have in mind, we have the perfect hot tub for you!

Planning a Cottage Holiday?

Are you planning to book a holiday cottage? We can deliver, install and pre-heat your hot tub so you can jump straight into it when you arrive. All we need is permission from the property owner and suitable access to water & electric and we'll do the rest. If you to hire a hot tub for your cottage holiday please call 07533 276099.

Sounds good, but what do I need to do?

Every hot tub from Hire A Hot Tub is delivered quickly and efficiently by our experts. And we can usually install your hot tub in around two hours or less. We deliver direct to your door and then carefully install it wherever you wish. Then we fill it with water, plug it in and that's it. You don't have to lift a finger!

What if it rains?

If you wish to be sheltered from the weather whether it be rain or sunshine or you just want a little extra privacy, we can supply a gazebo with your hot tub for a small fee. Offering you the protection or privacy you need.

What about the technical stuff?

Hiring hot tub from Hire A Hot Tub couldn't be simpler. Firstly, all you need is a clear space for your hot tub. It needs to be completely flat and 2.5m x 2.5m, 3m x 3m square if you are having a gazebo. Secondly, you'll also need a standard electric supply & water supply within reaching distance of where the hot tub is to be set up. Lastly, we need clear access, please ensure pathways are clear. If you have any doubts call us for advice on 07533 276099.

Is there anything else I need to know?

Your hot tub normally takes around 16 to 18 hours to reach the ideal operating temperature. Don't worry though, we'll give you full instructions on its use. Make sure that younger children are adequately supervised. Once the hire period is over our professional staff will arrive at an agreed time, empty the tub and leave your premises neat and tidy. Simple!

I'm nearly convinced! How much is it to hire my hot tub?

You can check our prices on our Facebook page or message us or call 07533 276099 to get a quote. We normally deliver Thursday or Friday for weekend hire, subject to availability. But if you need different days let us know which days suit you and we'll try to deliver around your schedule.

How do I book?

All we need is a booking/damage deposit to secure a booking which you can pay by cash, cheque, bank transfer or PayPal. This payment is separate from the hire fee and is refundable after the hire providing there is no damage to the equipment. Message us @hireahottub2000 or call 07533 276099.

I have other questions...

Please get in touch and we will do our very best to answer all your questions.
